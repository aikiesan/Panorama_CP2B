🔬 PROBLEM IDENTIFIED:

The database was using FCp as "available fraction" (0-1), but values were not
aligned with scientific literature. Key issues:

1. Sangue animal: FCp=0.85 (85% available) → WRONG
   Literature shows 60-75% goes to other uses (farinha, plasma)
   
2. Palha de cana: FCp=0.30 (30% available) → WRONG
   Literature shows 90% competition (soil protection + E2G)
   
3. Bagaço de cana: FCp=0.05 (5% available) → WRONG
   Should be ~18% after cogeração priority

🔬 METHODOLOGY VALIDATION:

Formula Used: SAF = FC × (1/FCp) × FS × FL

Where FCp is stored as "available fraction":
  - FCp = 0.20 means 20% available after competition
  - Equivalent to FCp_multiplier = 5.0 (alta competição)
  - Both representations are mathematically equivalent
